How it works

Three safe steps

  1. Post or search. Share the item, approximate location, and date, or browse recent reports.
  2. Verify ownership. Ask for unique details before giving out a found item.
  3. Meet safely. Choose a public place like a campus desk, station office, cafe, or community centre.
